Our Philosophy

ものづくりの
精神

Monozukuri — the spirit of making things. In Japan, this means total dedication to craft, continuous improvement, and an intolerance for defects. Hack JP believes cybersecurity deserves nothing less than this standard.

改

継続的改善

Kaizen · Continuous Improvement

Every vulnerability found is a lesson. Every incident is a system to improve. Security is never finished — only refined.

匠

職人精神

Shokunin · Master Craftsperson

The Japanese ideal of the master practitioner — someone who has devoted their life to a single discipline with total commitment.

和

調和と協力

Wa · Harmony & Collaboration

Japan's cybersecurity community is built on trust, information sharing, and collective responsibility — not competition alone.

守破離
Shu Ha Ri

Learn the rules. Break the rules. Transcend the rules. The three stages of mastery in Japanese martial arts — and in hacking.

Programs · プログラム

Structured for
mastery

Hack JP programmes follow the Japanese educational tradition of structured progression — each stage building completely on the last, with no shortcuts tolerated and no prerequisites skipped.

学ぶ

SECCON Partnership

Hack JP is the community home for SECCON — Japan's national CTF competition, one of Asia's most prestigious and technically demanding security events.

繋ぐ

Japan University Security Network

Connecting security clubs at Tokyo University, Keio, Waseda, Kyoto, and Osaka — building the academic-to-industry pipeline that Japanese industry needs.

守る

Critical Infrastructure Defence

A specialist programme focused on the cybersecurity of Japan's critical infrastructure — nuclear, transport, and financial systems, in coordination with NISC.

研究

Japan Bug Bounty League

A structured bug bounty programme connecting Japanese security researchers with major Japanese technology companies — keeping IP discovery within Japan.

育てる

女性ハッカー育成

A dedicated programme developing Japan's next generation of female security professionals — addressing one of the most significant talent gaps in Japanese cybersecurity.

繋がる

Japan ↔ APAC Bridge

Connecting Japanese security talent with regional opportunities in Singapore, South Korea, Australia, and Southeast Asia — and bringing the best of APAC to Japan.
